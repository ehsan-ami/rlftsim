## Model Checkpoints

We are happy to share the RLFTSim checkpoints used in our WOSAC submission. To request access, please reach out to `eahmadi at ualberta dot ca`. Due to Waymo's data usage policy, we ask that you provide a screenshot confirming your registration on the **My Submissions** page of the Waymo Open Dataset.

Our checkpoints are built on top of the SMART-tiny architecture. For inference, you can use the codebases from [CAT-K](https://github.com/NVlabs/catk) or [SMART](https://github.com/rainmaker22/SMART).
